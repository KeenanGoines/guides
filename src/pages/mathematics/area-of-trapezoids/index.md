## Area of Trapezoids

The area of a trapizoid can be calculated by doing base one plus base two divided by 2 times height.
A = ((b1 + b2) / 2) x h
An example would be base one is 2, base two is 2, and the height is 2 which would give you a height of:
((2 + 2) / 2) x 2 = 4
